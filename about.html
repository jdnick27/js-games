About 2D Golf

Use the left and right arrow keys to adjust your shot angle. The controls are invertedâ€”pressing left rotates the aim right and pressing right rotates it left. Press the Space key once to start the power meter and press again to launch the ball. Try to land it in the hole while avoiding hazards.

Press R to rehit from your previous spot. The shot you just took still counts and a penalty stroke is added unless the ball landed in the water or out of bounds.

Press N to restart the current hole from the tee without changing its layout.

The game features 18 holes played in sequence. Your strokes for each hole are shown on the scoreboard.
