About JS Games

JS Games is a collection of small browser games written in JavaScript.

The current lineup includes:

  • 2D Golf – aim the ball and try to finish 18 holes under par.
  • The Maze – navigate through a series of increasingly large mazes.
  • Baseball Game – time your swing to hit the pitched ball.
  • Stick Figure Fighting – defeat the AI opponent using simple moves.
  • Archery Darts – fire arrows at a dartboard to score points.
  • Roulette – place chips on numbers and spin the wheel.
  • Bowling Game – roll the ball at pins and score using bowling rules.
  • Solitaire – build up the four foundations to win.
